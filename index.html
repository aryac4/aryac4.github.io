One Direction

One Direction is a pop band based in London created by the X- Factor. The band consisted of Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, and Zayn Malik (until he left in 2015). When Zayn had left the band, One Direction dissolved around a couple years later. Though, they did fall apart, many fans still love One Direction.
